About US

Dreams should be something which never let you sleep, said Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam. Just like that there was a dream which made the nights of Mr.Sreenivasan Pillai & Mr.Sreejith sleepless. The dream to provide organic & hygienic juice products to people. Juices just like the nature has given; pure, organic & healthy. They were well aware of the health issues raised by the local juice selling stalls. They believed the people deserved more pure & healthy products. So they decided to get some sleep, by fulfilling their dream. that was the beginning of S R Traders in 2016.They quickly got into the minds of people with their delicious Sugarcane juices which came in two different flavors. The whole production under India's best food technology- 'CFTRI Technology for Beverage Products' helped their products to be fresh, pure & healthy. And they are living their dreams. But always dreams has to be big. The S R Traders will be launching two more food products by next two years. The tasty family is going to be big.
